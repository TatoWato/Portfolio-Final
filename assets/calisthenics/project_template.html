The Final Stop Regarding Calisthenics and Gymnastics Training!

Over the years I have become increasingly interested and fascinated by the potential of the human body. One of the greatest expressions of its capability comes in the form of calisthenics and gymnastics. This site is my attempt to celebrate this, by passing knowledge down to you.

The amount of information regarding fitness is so abundant that it has become overwhelming as well as frustrating. Furthermore, the information that is available is scattered across the web. My focus is to create a central resource in which all aspects of training can converge at a single point. Within, you will find a wealth of material such as:

  • Training Methodologies and Routines
  • Exercise Progressions
  • Recovery Modalities
  • Flexibility and Mobility Secrets
  • Nutrition Insights

With that said, welcome. Regardless of your goals, age, or skill level, you have a place here. May you find some wisdom within and I sincerely hope that I can inspire the same level of passion I have, within you.
