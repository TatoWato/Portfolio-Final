Where To Eat

Restaurants in Carolina and Kure Beach

Pleasure Island is home to some of the area's favorite restaurants, including award winning seafood as well as locally sourced fare. With options ranging from fresh oysters and peel-and-eat shrimp, to waterfront dining, breakfast diners, take-out, bistros, cafes, and sub shops plus pizza, donuts, ice cream and fudge, Pleasure Island is a popular dining destination for area residents and visitors alike. During the off season many of the restaurants offer great deals.

Carolina and Kure Beach on Pleasure Island offer a multitude of attractions and fun things to do for visitors and residents of all ages and types. Come for a vacation, come for the fun events and festivals, and discover all the unique and wonderful attractions we have on display!
